<p><p>The Oral Fast Dissolving Film market includes two main types: orodispersible films and oromucosal films. Orodispersible films dissolve quickly in the oral cavity, releasing medication without the need for water, making them ideal for patients with swallowing difficulties. Oromucosal films are designed for localized delivery, adhering to the mucosal membranes to provide sustained drug release, typically for conditions affecting the oral cavity. Both types offer convenience and improved bioavailability compared to traditional dosage forms.</p></p>
