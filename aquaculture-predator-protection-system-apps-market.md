Permanent systems are fixed installations, offering long-term protection for large-scale aquaculture farms, often integrated into infrastructure. Both types aim to safeguard fish stocks from predators like birds, seals, or otters, enhancing productivity and reducing losses in aquaculture operations.</p></p>
