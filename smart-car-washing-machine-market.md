<p><p>The Smart Car Washing Machine market is divided into two main types: contact and contactless washing systems. Contact systems employ brushes and cloths that physically touch the car's surface, effectively removing dirt and grime but potentially causing minor scratches. In contrast, contactless systems use high-pressure water jets and advanced cleaning solutions to clean vehicles without direct contact, minimizing the risk of surface damage. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, balancing thorough cleaning with care for vehicle paint.</p></p>
