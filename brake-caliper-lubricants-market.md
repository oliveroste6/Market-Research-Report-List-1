Silicone lubricants offer excellent thermal stability and resist moisture, making them ideal for high-temperature environments. Synthetic lubricants provide superior performance by ensuring better lubrication and longevity under extreme conditions. Anti-seize lubricants prevent corrosion and facilitate disassembly, enhancing the lifespan of brake components.
