<p><strong>The Automatic Blast Cabinet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressure Blast Cabinet</li><li>Suction Blast Cabinet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic blast cabinet market primarily comprises two types: pressure blast cabinets and suction blast cabinets. Pressure blast cabinets utilize compressed air to propel abrasive material at high velocities, ensuring efficient and uniform surface treatment. In contrast, suction blast cabinets use a vacuum system to draw abrasives from a storage container and accelerate them towards the workpiece, offering gentler abrasive action. Both types are used for cleaning, smoothing, and polishing surfaces in various industries, enhancing productivity and finish quality.</p></p>
