<p><p>The hardware token market encompasses three main types: USB tokens, SIM tokens, and mini tokens. USB tokens are compact devices plugged into a computer's USB port, providing authentication for secure access. SIM tokens integrate with mobile devices, leveraging SIM cards for two-factor authentication, enhancing mobile security. Mini tokens are small, portable devices that generate one-time passwords (OTPs) for secure transactions. These various hardware tokens offer distinct authentication methods, catering to diverse security needs across different applications and industries.</p></p>
