<p><strong>The Mooring Inspection Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Below Water Inspection (BWI)</li><li>Above Water Inspection (AWI)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mooring Inspection market is segmented into Below Water Inspection (BWI) and Above Water Inspection (AWI). BWI involves underwater assessments of mooring systems, examining components like chains, buoys, and anchors for wear or damage, typically using divers or remotely operated vehicles. AWI focuses on visible components above the waterline, inspecting mooring structures, fittings, and connection points for structural integrity and safety. Both segments are crucial for ensuring the reliability and performance of mooring systems in marine environments.</p></p>
