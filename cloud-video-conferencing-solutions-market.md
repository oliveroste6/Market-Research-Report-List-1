<p><ul><li>Scalable Video Coding (SVC) Type</li><li>Advanced Video Coding (AVC) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cloud Video Conferencing Solutions offer two primary market types based on video encoding technologies: Scalable Video Coding (SVC) and Advanced Video Coding (AVC). SVC allows for adaptive streaming by enabling video to be encoded at multiple quality levels, providing flexibility for varying network conditions and device capabilities. In contrast, AVC is a standard video compression format that offers efficient bandwidth usage but lacks the scalability features of SVC. Both cater to different user needs in video conferencing applications.</p></p>
