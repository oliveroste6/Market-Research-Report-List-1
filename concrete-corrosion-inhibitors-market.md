<p><p>The concrete corrosion inhibitors market is categorized into three main types: inorganic, organic, and polymer inhibitors. Inorganic corrosion inhibitors, such as calcium nitrite and sodium silicate, work by providing protective layers or altering the chemical composition of concrete. Organic inhibitors, derived from plant or synthetic sources, offer a more eco-friendly approach by slowing down corrosion processes. Polymer inhibitors utilize synthetic polymers to enhance the durability and longevity of concrete, providing a barrier against environmental aggressors. Each type serves to enhance concrete resilience.</p></p>
