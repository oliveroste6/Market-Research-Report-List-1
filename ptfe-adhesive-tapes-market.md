Medium-density tapes provide enhanced abrasion resistance, suitable for demanding applications. High-density tapes are the most robust, designed for high-temperature and chemical-resistant environments. Each type caters to specific needs across industries like electrical, automotive, and healthcare, maximizing efficiency and durability.</p></p>
