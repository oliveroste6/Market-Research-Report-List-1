<p><p>The light curtain sensor market is categorized into two primary types: active and passive. Active light curtain sensors emit a beam of infrared or laser light, detecting interruptions to identify objects or personnel within a protected area, thus ensuring safety and automation in various industries. In contrast, passive light curtain sensors rely on the detection of changes in light levels, such as shadows, to trigger responses. Both types are integral to enhancing workplace safety and efficiency in manufacturing and logistics environments.</p></p>
