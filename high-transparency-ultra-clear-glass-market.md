<p><strong>The High Transparency Ultra Clear Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rolled Glass</li><li>Float Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The High Transparency Ultra Clear Glass market primarily includes two types: rolled glass and float glass. Rolled glass is manufactured by passing molten glass through rollers, creating a consistent thickness, ideal for applications where flexibility and shaping are required. Float glass, on the other hand, is produced by floating molten glass on molten tin, resulting in a flawless surface and uniform thickness. Both types are prized for their clarity and minimal distortion, making them suitable for architectural, automotive, and decorative applications.</p></p>
