<p><p>Pneumatic suspension systems use air springs to provide a smooth ride and adjustable height. The **Capsule** type features a compact, cylindrical air spring, ideal for limited spaces and light-duty applications. The **Membrane** type uses a flexible rubber membrane, offering durability and versatility for medium-duty vehicles. The **Compound** type combines elements of both, integrating a piston and membrane for enhanced load-bearing capacity and stability, making it suitable for heavy-duty and commercial vehicles. Each type caters to specific performance and space requirements.</p></p>
