<p><p>The imaging diagnostic catheters market includes various specialized types used for visualizing and diagnosing medical conditions. Angiography catheters are employed to visualize blood vessels and diagnose cardiovascular diseases. Ultrasound catheters utilize ultrasound technology for real-time imaging, often in minimally invasive procedures. Optical coherence tomography (OCT) catheters provide high-resolution imaging of tissues, commonly used in ophthalmology and cardiology. Electrophysiology (EP) catheters measure electrical activity in the heart, aiding in arrhythmia diagnosis. Other market segments may include catheters for unique imaging techniques or applications.</p></p>
