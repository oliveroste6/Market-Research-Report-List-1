<p><p>The smart LED bulb market features three primary connectivity types: ZigBee, Wi-Fi, and Bluetooth. ZigBee operates on a mesh network, enhancing inter-device communication and energy efficiency, making it ideal for smart home systems. Wi-Fi bulbs connect directly to home networks, allowing for easy control via smartphones and voice assistants but may consume more power. Bluetooth bulbs offer a simpler, more localized control without the need for a hub, ideal for smaller setups. Each type caters to different user preferences and technical requirements.</p></p>
