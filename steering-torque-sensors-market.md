<p><ul><li>Contact Type</li><li>Non-contact Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steering torque sensors market is categorized into two main types: contact and non-contact sensors. Contact type sensors measure torque through physical contact with steering components, providing precise feedback but may involve wear over time. Non-contact type sensors utilize magnetic or optical methods to detect torque without direct contact, offering higher durability and reduced maintenance. Both types are essential for advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS) and electric power steering (EPS), driving innovations in vehicle safety and performance.</p></p>
