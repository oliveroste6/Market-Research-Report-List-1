<p><p>The Wet Desiccation Atmospheric Water Generator (AWG) is an innovative technology designed to extract moisture from the air and convert it into potable water using a desiccant material. This process involves the absorption of humidity from the atmosphere, followed by the release of the collected water through a heating mechanism. The Wet Desiccation method offers energy-efficient solutions, particularly in areas with high humidity, making it a sustainable alternative for water generation.</p><p>The Wet Desiccation AWG market is anticipated to experience robust growth, with an expected CAGR of 6.5% during the forecast period.
