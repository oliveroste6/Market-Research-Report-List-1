<p><strong>The Textile Screen Print Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In-line</li><li>Carousel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Textile screen print machines are primarily categorized into two types: in-line and carousel. In-line machines are designed for continuous production, facilitating high-speed operations by allowing multiple screens to print simultaneously as fabric moves through. Carousel machines, on the other hand, feature rotating platters where screens are applied one at a time. This allows greater flexibility for designs and fabric types but usually operates at a slower pace. Each type serves distinct production needs and volume requirements in the textile industry.</p></p>
