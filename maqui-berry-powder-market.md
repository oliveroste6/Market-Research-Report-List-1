<p><p>The Maqui Berry Powder market is primarily segmented into two types based on drying methods: spray drying and freeze drying. Spray drying involves rapidly converting liquid Maqui berry extracts into powder through a heated air stream, preserving color and nutrients while offering cost-effectiveness. Freeze drying, on the other hand, involves freezing the berries and then removing moisture under vacuum, which retains more flavor and nutrients. Both methods cater to different consumer preferences and applications in the food and health sectors.</p></p>
