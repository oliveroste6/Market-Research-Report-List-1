<p><p>Power semiconductor test systems are essential for evaluating semiconductor devices' performance and reliability. The dynamic test system focuses on assessing the device's behavior under varying power conditions, simulating real-world operational scenarios and transient responses. In contrast, the static test system measures characteristics at fixed conditions, such as voltage and current levels, providing insights into the device's fundamental properties. Together, these systems ensure comprehensive testing to guarantee the performance and durability of power semiconductor components in various applications.</p></p>
