<p><ul><li>Rolled Glass</li><li>Float Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Super White Glass market is primarily categorized into rolled glass and float glass. Rolled glass is produced by passing molten glass through rollers, resulting in a textured surface and varying thicknesses, making it ideal for applications like manufacturing windows and doors. Float glass, on the other hand, is created by floating molten glass on molten tin, yielding a smooth, uniform, and high-transparency product. Both types are widely used in construction, automotive, and interior design industries for their aesthetic and functional qualities.</p></p>
