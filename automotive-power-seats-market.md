<p><p>The automotive power seats market is categorized into three main types: Single Motor Type, Double Motor Type, and Others. The Single Motor Type utilizes a single motor to adjust both the seat position and the backrest, making it a cost-effective solution for basic functionality. The Double Motor Type features separate motors for seat adjustment and backrest recline, providing enhanced comfort and flexibility. The "Others" category includes advanced systems with additional features such as memory settings and massage functions, catering to premium vehicle segments.</p></p>
