<p><strong>The Conventional Ultrasonic Testing Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Through Transmission</li><li>Pulse Echo</li><li>Phased Array Pulse Echo</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Conventional Ultrasonic Testing (UT) machines utilize different techniques for material inspection. Through Transmission involves sending ultrasonic waves through a test specimen to detect flaws, with receivers on the opposite side. Pulse Echo uses a single transducer to emit pulses and receive echoes, providing insights into internal structures and defects. Phased Array Pulse Echo employs multiple transducers to focus and steer ultrasonic beams, allowing for detailed imaging and enhanced defect detection in complex geometries. Each method serves specific inspection needs in various industries.</p></p>
