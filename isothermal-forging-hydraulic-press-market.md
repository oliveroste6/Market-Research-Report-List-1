<p><p>The isothermal forging hydraulic press market features two main types: combination frame hydraulic presses and integral frame hydraulic presses. Combination frame hydraulic presses consist of separate structures for the ram and frame, allowing for flexibility in design and customization. In contrast, integral frame hydraulic presses have a unified structure, offering enhanced stability and compactness. Both types are crucial for isothermal forging processes, ensuring precise control of temperature and pressure to achieve desired material properties in manufacturing applications.</p></p>
