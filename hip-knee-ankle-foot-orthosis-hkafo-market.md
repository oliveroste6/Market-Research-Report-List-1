<p><p>The HKAFO (Hip Knee Ankle Foot Orthosis) market is categorized into dynamic and static orthotics. Dynamic orthotics are designed to promote mobility by allowing natural movement while providing support, ideal for individuals with muscle weakness or neurological conditions. In contrast, static orthotics are more rigid, focusing on immobilization and stability for conditions requiring limited movement, such as severe paralysis or post-surgery recovery. Both types serve essential roles in aiding mobility and function for individuals with varied physical disabilities.</p></p>
