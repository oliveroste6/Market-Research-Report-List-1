<p><p>The Great Animal Imaging market encompasses various imaging modalities that aid in veterinary diagnostics and research. Optical imaging utilizes light to visualize biological processes, while radionuclide imaging employs radioactive tracers for functional insights. MRI provides detailed soft tissue imaging using magnetic fields, and computed tomography (CT) delivers cross-sectional views for precise diagnosis. Ultrasound imaging utilizes sound waves for real-time visualization of internal structures. Each technique contributes uniquely to animal health monitoring, disease detection, and preclinical studies.</p></p>
