<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Power Steering Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Blade Type Steering Pump</li><li>Gear Type Steering Pump</li><li>Plunger Type Steering Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The **Blade Type Steering Pump** uses rotating blades to generate hydraulic pressure, offering smooth operation and efficiency, commonly found in modern vehicles. The **Gear Type Steering Pump** employs meshing gears to create pressure, known for its durability and simplicity, often used in heavy-duty applications. The **Plunger Type Steering Pump** utilizes reciprocating plungers for precise pressure control, providing high performance and reliability, typically in advanced or high-end vehicles. Each type caters to specific needs, balancing efficiency, durability, and performance in automotive power steering systems.</p></p>
