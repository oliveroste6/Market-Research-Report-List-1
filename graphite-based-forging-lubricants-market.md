<p><ul><li>Hot Forging Lubricants</li><li>Cold Forging Lubricants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Graphite-based forging lubricants are essential in the manufacturing process to reduce friction and wear between metal surfaces. The market is divided into hot forging lubricants and cold forging lubricants. Hot forging lubricants are utilized in high-temperature environments, enabling smooth metal deformation while preventing oxidation and scale formation. In contrast, cold forging lubricants are designed for lower temperatures, allowing for precise metal shaping with minimal surface damage. Both types enhance tool life, improve surface finish, and ensure efficient production.</p></p>
