<p><p>The High Speed Drug Dispensing Machine market is categorized into two main types: freestanding and embedded drug dispensing machines. Freestanding machines operate independently, allowing flexibility in placement and usage in various healthcare settings. They are typically larger and can handle higher volumes of medication.
