<p><p>The healthcare plastics market includes various types, each suited for specific applications. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is widely used for medical tubing and bags due to its flexibility. PE (Polyethylene) is favored for its chemical resistance, often used in packaging. PP (Polypropylene) is valued for its toughness in surgical instruments. PS (Polystyrene) is used in disposable items. ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene) is known for its strength in durable products. PC (Polycarbonate) offers transparency in medical devices. PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) is used for its non-stick properties, while TPU (Thermoplastic Polyurethane) provides elasticity for flexible applications.</p></p>
