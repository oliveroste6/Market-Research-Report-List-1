<p><p>The electric razor market is primarily divided into two types: foil and rotary razors. Foil razors feature a straight, thin blade covered by a perforated foil which allows for a close, precise shave. They excel in providing smooth results on flat surfaces, making them ideal for individuals with fine hair. Conversely, rotary razors use circular blades that adapt to facial contours, offering flexibility and comfort for varied hair types. Each type caters to different shaving preferences and routines.</p></p>
