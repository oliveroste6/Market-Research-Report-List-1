<p><strong>The Baseboard Heater Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electric</li><li>Hydronic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The baseboard heater market comprises two primary types: electric and hydronic. Electric baseboard heaters use electrical resistance to generate heat, providing a convenient and easy-to-install option for individual rooms. They offer precise temperature control and require no water or piping. In contrast, hydronic baseboard heaters circulate heated water from a boiler through pipes, offering efficient and consistent warmth. While hydronic systems involve more complex installation, they provide higher thermal comfort and lower operational costs over time.</p></p>
