<p><ul><li>PVPP/R-PVPP</li><li>Silica Gel</li><li>Papain (Proteolytic Enzyme)</li><li>Others (Bentonite, Tannic Acid)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The beer stabilizers market encompasses various types used to enhance the clarity and shelf-life of beer. PVPP (Polyvinylpolypyrrolidone) and R-PVPP are synthetic polymers that help remove unwanted proteins and polyphenols. Silica gel acts as a filtration aid, trapping particles. Papain, a proteolytic enzyme, aids in protein breakdown, preventing haze formation. Other stabilizers like bentonite, a clay, and tannic acid, derived from plant sources, also contribute to beer clarification and stability, ensuring a high-quality final product.</p></p>
