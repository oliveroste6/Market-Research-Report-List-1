<p><strong>The Home Boilers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-condensing Boilers</li><li>Condensing Boilers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The home boilers market includes two primary types: non-condensing and condensing boilers. Non-condensing boilers operate at lower efficiency, expelling exhaust gases without recovering heat, making them less eco-friendly. In contrast, condensing boilers are designed to capture and reuse heat from exhaust gases, significantly enhancing energy efficiency and reducing emissions. As environmental regulations tighten and energy costs rise, the trend is shifting towards condensing boilers, which offer better performance and sustainability for residential heating needs.</p></p>
