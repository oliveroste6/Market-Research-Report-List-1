<p><p>The robotic grippers market encompasses various types, including electric, pneumatic, vacuum, and magnetic grippers. Electric grippers utilize motors for precise control and are favored for their high reliability and torque. Pneumatic grippers use compressed air, offering speed and strength, ideal for heavy payloads. Vacuum grippers employ suction cups to hold irregularly shaped objects, making them versatile for fragile items. Magnetic grippers utilize magnets for securely lifting ferrous materials, enhancing efficiency in assembly lines and industrial applications.</p></p>
