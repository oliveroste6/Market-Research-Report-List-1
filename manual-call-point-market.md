<p><p>Manual call points are essential components of fire alarm systems, designed to enable individuals to manually trigger an alarm in emergency situations. The market for manual call points can be categorized into two main types: wired and wireless. Wired manual call points are hardwired into the alarm system, ensuring constant connectivity and reliability. In contrast, wireless manual call points offer flexibility and easier installation since they communicate with the alarm system via radio signals, making them ideal for retrofitting or temporary setups.</p></p>
