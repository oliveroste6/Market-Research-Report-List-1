<p><p>The Liquid Leak Detection Sensors market is categorized into two main types: invasive and non-invasive leak detection. Invasive leak detection involves the installation of sensors that may require physical alteration of equipment to monitor leaks directly within pipes or systems. Conversely, non-invasive leak detection utilizes external sensors that can identify leaks without disturbing the system, often using acoustic or thermal methods. Both types aim to enhance safety and efficiency in various industries by providing timely detection of liquid leaks.</p></p>
