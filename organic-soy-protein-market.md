<p><p>The organic soy protein market includes three main types: soy protein concentrates, soy protein isolates, and soy protein flour. Soy protein concentrates have 70% protein and are often used in meat alternatives and dairy substitutes. Soy protein isolates contain around 90% protein, providing high purity for nutritional supplements and functional foods. Soy protein flour, with lower protein content, is utilized in baking and as a filler in various food products. Each type serves distinct applications while enhancing food's nutritional profile.</p></p>
