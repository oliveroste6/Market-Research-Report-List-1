<p><strong>The Low Light Imager Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CCD</li><li>CMOS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The low light imager market primarily features two types of imaging technologies: Charge-Coupled Devices (CCD) and Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor (CMOS) sensors. CCD sensors are known for their high-quality image reproduction and superior light sensitivity, making them ideal for applications requiring outstanding low-light performance. Conversely, CMOS sensors are gaining popularity due to their lower power consumption, faster processing speeds, and integration capabilities, making them suitable for a wide range of consumer and industrial applications in low-light conditions.</p></p>
