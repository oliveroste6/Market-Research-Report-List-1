<p><ul><li>Active Harmonic Filter</li><li>Passive Harmonic Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The harmonic filter market comprises two primary types: active and passive harmonic filters. Active harmonic filters use advanced electronic components to actively detect and counteract harmonic distortions in electrical systems, offering real-time compensation and superior performance. In contrast, passive harmonic filters employ passive components like inductors and capacitors to mitigate harmonics at specific frequencies, providing a cost-effective solution but with limited adaptability. Both types are essential for enhancing power quality and ensuring compliance with electrical standards in various industries.</p></p>
