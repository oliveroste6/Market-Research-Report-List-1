<p><p>Level probes are essential instruments used to measure the level of substances, either solids or liquids. The market is primarily divided into two types: point level probes and continuous level probes. Point level probes detect specific thresholds, providing alerts when levels reach a defined point, suitable for applications like overflow prevention. Continuous level probes, on the other hand, measure levels continuously, providing real-time data and facilitating efficient monitoring and control in various industries such as chemicals, food and beverage, and water treatment.</p></p>
