<p><p>The poultry plucking machine market can be categorized into vertical and horizontal types based on their design and operation. Vertical plucking machines are characterized by their upright orientation, allowing for gravity-assisted plucking, which can enhance efficiency and reduce labor. In contrast, horizontal plucking machines operate in a flat orientation, providing a more stable platform for processing larger batches of poultry. Both types cater to different processing needs, with vertical models often preferred for smaller operations and horizontal models suitable for larger facilities.</p></p>
