<p><p>The Small Cell 5G Network market is categorized into three main types: picocells, femtocells, and microcells. Picocells are designed for small coverage areas, typically serving dense urban environments or specific venues. Femtocells connect to a broadband network to provide localized coverage in residences or small businesses. Microcells serve larger areas than picocells, ideal for urban centers and areas with higher traffic. Together, these technologies enhance network capacity, improve coverage, and enable efficient data transmission in diverse environments.</p></p>
