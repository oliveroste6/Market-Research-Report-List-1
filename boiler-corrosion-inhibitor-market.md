<p><p>The Boiler Corrosion Inhibitor Market is segmented into two main types: organic and inorganic corrosion inhibitors. Organic corrosion inhibitors are typically derived from carbon-based compounds and are favored for their effectiveness in preventing corrosion through film formation and passivation. In contrast, inorganic corrosion inhibitors often consist of mineral-based compounds and function by altering the corrosive environment, providing resistance against corrosion. Both types play crucial roles in prolonging boiler life and enhancing operational efficiency in various industrial applications.</p></p>
