<p><p>Liver Function Tests (LFTs) comprise various assessments to evaluate liver health. Key tests include Alanine Aminotransferase (ALT) and Aspartate Aminotransferase (AST), which measure enzyme levels indicating liver damage. Alkaline Phosphatase (ALP) assesses bile duct function, while Gamma GT evaluates bile flow and liver disease. Total Bilirubin measures liver's ability to process waste, and Albumin reflects protein synthesis capability. Other tests may include prothrombin time and lactate dehydrogenase, providing a comprehensive view of liver function and overall health.</p></p>
