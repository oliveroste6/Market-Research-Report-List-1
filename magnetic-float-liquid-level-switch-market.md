<p><p>The Magnetic Float Liquid Level Switch market features two primary types: top-mounted and side-mounted. Top-mounted types are installed at the tank's top, offering easy access and installation while accurately detecting liquid levels in various applications. Side-mounted types, conversely, are affixed to the tank's side, making them suitable for larger tanks or environments with constrained top access. Both types utilize a magnetic float mechanism to signal liquid levels, ensuring reliable monitoring in industrial and residential settings.</p></p>
