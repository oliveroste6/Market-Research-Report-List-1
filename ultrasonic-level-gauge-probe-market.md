<p><p>The ultrasonic level gauge probe market features two primary types: point-type and continuous ultrasonic level gauge probes. Point-type ultrasonic liquid level meters measure levels at specific points, providing discrete data, ideal for applications where precise, periodic measurement is sufficient. In contrast, continuous ultrasonic level gauge probes offer real-time monitoring of liquid levels, producing constant data streams suited for applications requiring ongoing oversight. Both technologies leverage ultrasonic waves to determine levels, delivering efficiency and accuracy across various industries.</p></p>
