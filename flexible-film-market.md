<p><p>The flexible film market comprises various types, including Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene (BOPP), Cast Polypropylene (CPP), and Polyester films. BOPP films are known for their clarity, strength, and moisture resistance, making them suitable for packaging applications. CPP films offer excellent sealing properties and flexibility, often used in food packaging. Polyester films are recognized for their durability and thermal stability, widely employed in labels and industrial applications. Other films in the market include various specialty and biodegradable options catering to diverse packaging needs.</p></p>
