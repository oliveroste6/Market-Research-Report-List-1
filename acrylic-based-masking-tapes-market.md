<p><p>Acrylic-based masking tapes are categorized into two main types: single-sided and double-sided. Single-sided masking tapes have adhesive on one side, ideal for applications like painting, surface protection, and general masking tasks. They offer easy handling and clean removal. Double-sided masking tapes, on the other hand, feature adhesive on both sides, making them suitable for mounting, bonding, and securing objects together. Both types provide strong adhesion, durability, and resistance to environmental factors, catering to diverse industrial and DIY needs.</p></p>
