<p><p>The eco fibres market is divided into two main types: natural fibres and synthetic fibres. Synthetic fibres, on the other hand, are man-made, typically from petrochemicals, and include materials like polyester and nylon. While they offer durability and cost-effectiveness, concerns about their environmental impact drive a growing demand for more sustainable alternatives within the market.</p></p>
