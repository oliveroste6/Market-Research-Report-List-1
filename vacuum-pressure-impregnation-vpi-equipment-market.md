<p><p>Vacuum Pressure Impregnation (VPI) Equipment is utilized for enhancing the durability and performance of porous materials, primarily in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and electrical. The result is a stronger, more reliable product that can withstand harsh environments.</p><p>The VPI Equipment Market is witnessing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for high-performance materials and efficient manufacturing processes.
