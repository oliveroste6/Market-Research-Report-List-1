<p><p>X-ray Photoelectron Spectroscopy (XPS) is a surface-sensitive analytical technique used to determine the elemental composition and chemical states of materials. By irradiating a specimen with X-rays and measuring the kinetic energy of emitted photoelectrons, XPS provides detailed information about the surface chemistry of solids or thin films. This method is widely employed in fields such as materials science, nanotechnology, and surface engineering due to its ability to analyze the surface and interfaces of materials at the atomic level.</p><p>The X-ray Photoelectron Spectroscopy Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.6% during the forecast period.
