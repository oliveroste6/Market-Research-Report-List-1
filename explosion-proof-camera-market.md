<p><p>The explosion-proof camera market is primarily categorized into two types: integrated explosion-proof cameras and split-type explosion-proof cameras. Integrated explosion-proof cameras feature a single unit design that combines all necessary components within a durable casing, allowing for easy installation and reduced space requirements. Conversely, split-type explosion-proof cameras consist of separate components, such as the camera and lens, which can be positioned remotely, providing greater flexibility for challenging installation environments. Both types are essential for ensuring safety in hazardous locations.</p></p>
