<p><p>The ultra-thin flexible PCB market is categorized into single-layer and multi-layer types. Single-layer PCBs consist of a single conductive layer, making them lightweight and cost-effective, ideal for simple applications. In contrast, multi-layer PCBs feature several stacked layers, allowing for more complex circuit designs and higher component density, suitable for advanced electronics. The choice between these types depends on factors like application complexity, space constraints, and overall device performance requirements, driving diverse usage in various industries.</p></p>
