<p><p>The inkjet packaging and labeling market encompasses various types, including wide-format inkjet, narrow-web inkjet, and UV inkjet. Wide-format inkjet is ideal for large-scale prints and displays, commonly used in banners and signage. Narrow-web inkjet focuses on smaller labels and packaging, facilitating customization and short runs. UV inkjet utilizes ultraviolet light to cure ink instantly, enabling high-quality printing on diverse substrates, including rigid materials. Each type serves unique applications, enhancing efficiency and creative possibilities in packaging solutions.</p></p>
