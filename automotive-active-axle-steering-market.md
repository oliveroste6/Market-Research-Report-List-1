<p><ul><li>Active Front Axle Steering</li><li>Active Rear Axle Steering</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive active axle steering market consists of two main types: Active Front Axle Steering (AFAS) and Active Rear Axle Steering (ARAS). AFAS enhances vehicle maneuverability by electronically adjusting the angle of the front wheels, improving handling during turns and stability at high speeds. Conversely, ARAS adjusts the rear wheel angles to enhance steering response, stability, and agility, especially during low-speed maneuvers and tight corners. Both systems contribute to improved vehicle dynamics and overall driving experience.</p></p>
