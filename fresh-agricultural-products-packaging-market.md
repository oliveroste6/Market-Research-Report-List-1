<p><p>The Fresh Agricultural Products Packaging market includes several key types. Vacuum Skin Packaging (VSP) tightly seals products in a plastic skin, enhancing presentation and protection. Vacuum Thermoformed Packaging (VTP) uses heat to shape plastic around products, providing secure and efficient packaging.
