<p><p>Structural Health Monitoring (SHM) systems are essential for assessing the condition of structures. The market is divided into two main types: wired and wireless SHM systems. Wired SHM systems use physical cables to connect sensors to data acquisition units, providing reliable data transmission and power supply, but may be limited by installation complexity. In contrast, wireless SHM systems utilize wireless communication technologies, offering flexibility and easier installation, but may face challenges related to battery life and data security.</p></p>
