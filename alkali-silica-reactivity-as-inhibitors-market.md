<p><p>The Alkali Silica Reactivity (AS) inhibitors market is divided into two main types based on calcium chloride content. AS inhibitors containing calcium chloride are typically used to mitigate the expansion caused by alkali-silica reactions in concrete, enhancing durability. Both types aim to improve the longevity and safety of concrete structures.</p></p>
