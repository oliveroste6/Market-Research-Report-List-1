<p><p>The Luteinizing Hormone Test Kit market comprises various types, notably Time-resolved Immunofluorescence (TRIF) and Chemiluminescence Immunoassay (CLIA). TRIF utilizes fluorescent labels to detect hormone levels with high sensitivity and specificity, offering rapid results. In contrast, CLIA employs chemiluminescent reactions, providing enhanced sensitivity and a wide dynamic range for quantifying luteinizing hormone. Both methods facilitate precise hormonal analysis, playing a crucial role in diagnosing reproductive health issues and monitoring hormonal therapies. Each type caters to specific clinical needs and laboratory capabilities.</p></p>
