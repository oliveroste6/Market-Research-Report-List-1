<p><strong>The Fire-proof Hydraulic Fluid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HFA</li><li>HFB</li><li>HFC</li><li>HFD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fire-proof hydraulic fluid market is categorized into four main types: HFA, HFB, HFC, and HFD. HFA fluids are water-containing solutions, providing minimal flammability. HFB fluids are emulsions that contain a higher proportion of petroleum oils and possess improved fire resistance. HFC fluids are primarily water-based and offer good fire resistance along with lubrication properties. HFD fluids are non-water-containing liquids, typically made from synthetic materials, providing robust thermal stability and superior fire resistance, making them suitable for high-temperature applications.</p></p>
